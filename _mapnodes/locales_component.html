The locales are not required if you use the built in locales. Though if you do not use the builtin locales, it is easy to add locales for your map.

Where you can place locales

  • In the map's description field.
  • Item's name field.
  • Item's lore field.
  • Books's title field.
  • Books's page fields.
  • Region's message field.

Builtin Locales

Their are built in locales to help aid you with your map's message. The goal for built in locales are generic messages that can be applied to multiple maps. Their are locales for items, regions, generic message for game modes, and generic descriptions.

Key Value
item.space.helmet Space Helmet
region.enter.spawn You may not enter the opponent's team spawn.
region.enter.room You may not enter this room.
region.enter.point You may not enter past this point.
region.enter.hallway You may not enter past this hallway.
region.exit.room You may not exit this room.
region.exit.point You may not exit past this point.
region.exit.hallway You may not exit past this hallway.
region.exit.map You may not exit the map's playing field.
region.build.spawn You may not build in the spawn.
region.build.region You may not build in this region.
region.build.room You may not build in this room.
region.build.hallway You may not build in this hallway.
region.build.void You may not build in the void.
region.destroy.spawn You may not destroy in the spawn.
region.destroy.region You may not destroy in this region.
region.destroy.room You may not destroy in this room.
region.destroy.hallway You may not destroy in this hallway.
